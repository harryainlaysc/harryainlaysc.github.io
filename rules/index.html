RULES

TOTT Rules

GENERAL RULES

1. Attendance for each event is at 12:15. Meet your team’s host and show them your Student ID card to check in. Teams must have a minimum of eight team members present to participate.
2. All team members must have their Student Council fees paid before the first event. Those who fail to comply will not be allowed to participate.
3. Events will begin promptly at 12:20 each day.
4. It is the captain’s responsibility to make sure that their team members are aware of the rules, including the rules specific to each event.
5. All participants are expected to display behaviour that is consistent with the Titan virtues.
6. Tournament Management has the right to eliminate any individual whose behaviour interferes with the event.

SPIRIT POINTS

1. All team members are dressed according to their team’s theme 3 points per event
2. 100% attendance from a team for all events during the accumulation of points (the first five days) 10 points
3. The team’s Teacher Sponsor is present at an event 1 point per event
4. The team’s Teacher Sponsor is dressed according to their team’s theme 1 point per event
5. Tournament Management has the right to deduct points for actions or behaviours that do not display Titan virtues (i.e., cheating does not display honesty, etc.) 5 points per infraction
6. Likewise, Tournament Management has the right to award points for action or behaviours that do display Titan virtues 5 points
7. Points may be awarded or deducted based on a team’s behaviour towards their Teacher Sponsor, their host, Tournament Management and other event supervisors (i.e., did you thank your Teacher Sponsor and your host?)

Day 5: Minute to Win It

1. Meet your host in the gym for attendance before 12:15.
2. This event is your last chance to earn points to qualify for the quarter finals and to win the spirit prize. The top eight teams will move on to the quarter finals, and the bottom four will be eliminated from the tournament.
3. For the Minute to Win It challenges, your host will take your team through the 12 stations. At each station, the station manager will briefly explain the rules and prepare the player for the challenge.
4. Once the timer begins, players will have one minute to complete the challenge. If the challenge is successfully completed, the team will be awarded 3 points. If the challenge is not completed, the team will not be awarded any points.
5. Team members are encouraged to cheer on their teammates but are not allowed to help them.
6. Please keep in mind that this event must run on schedule in order for all teams to have the chance to complete all twelve stations. Teams will rotate every two minutes. This means that at each station, one minute will be spent on the challenge, and another will be spent resetting the materials and switching stations.
7. Please have the order in which your team members will compete determined before the event to save time in between stations. Since there are twelve stations, each team member must participate in at least one station.
8. The Challenges are:
Stack Attack: 36 cups must be stacked into a self-standing pyramid (beginning with a row of 8, then a row of 7, then 6, etc.) *more than one player is allowed*
Cookie Monster: the player must move an oreo placed on their forehead into their mouth without without using their hands. Each time it falls, the minute will be shortened by five seconds.
This Blows: the player must knock over 10 cups using only the air from one balloon.
One-Handed Bracelet: the player must thread 25 cheerios onto a pipe cleaner and fasten it to their wrist using only one hand.
Movin’ On Up: there will be a stack on 40 cups, with one of a different colour on the top. The player must move the cups one at a time from the top of the stack to the bottom until the different-coloured cup is on top again. If they move more than one cup at a time, 3 cups will be added as a penalty for each cup moved out of turn.
Cotton Ball Scoop: the player will be blindfolded. In one bowl, there will be 20 cotton balls. They will have to move all 20 of them to another bowl with a spoon.
Pudding Suck-Up: the player will have a minute to eat the pudding with a straw, which can be used to suck up the pudding or to scoop it.
Rudolph Toss: the player will be given a string with a pom pom attached to one end, and a knot on the other. A dab of vaseline will be put on the tip of their nose. With the knotted end of the string in their mouth, they must get the pom pom to stick to their nose five times without using their hands.
Marshmallow Suck: the player will have to move 20 marshmallows from a plate to a cup using only the suction of a straw.
Bite Me: the player will have to move 8 paper bags from the floor to a table one at a time using only their mouth. The only body part allowed to touch the floor is their feet.
Matchmaker: on one table, there will be 18 small objects of 3 different colours placed underneath of 18 cups. The player must move the objects one at a time to the second table, where there will be 3 cups to sort the objects into.
Eat It Up: the player will have to eat an unrolled fruit roll-up (like a fruit-by-the-foot) without their hands.
9. If the player is cheating on a challenge, the team will not be awarded any points for it, regardless of whether they finish it.
10. Results will be posted on Tuesday evening, and captains will be contacted with further information for the quarter finals.
